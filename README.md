An Ai model which automatically checks the severity of the patients condition within 60 seconds of them entering the emergency room with the. help of a microphone and camera. The Problem: In overcroded emergnecy rooms nurses often spend crucial minutes entering data such as vitals manually in a computer instead of watching over patients in critical condition causing their condition to have a invisible declinign ultimately negatively affecting the patients Solution : My solution is to build an ai model with a fusion layer which gathers data about a patient from a microphone and camera using audio and visual cues to decide whether the patient needs immidiate attention or not all within 60 seconds of them entering into the room.
